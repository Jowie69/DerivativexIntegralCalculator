Integral and Derivative Calculator

by Mary Grace A. Asis
 
Use the following commands:
"d -expression to derive [ex. d 5x^2]
"i -expression to integrate (anti-derive) [ex. i 10y^3]
"c -new text color [ex. c lightblue]
"bg -new background color [ex. bg white]
"print -text to print [ex. print Derivative Calculator]
"clear *clears CLI*
"contact *prints author's id*
"facebook *opens author's facebook profile*
"test *testing command*
"help *show commands list*
